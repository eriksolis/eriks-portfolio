Hey there :wave: --- I'm Erik, a Game Developer / Game Designer from the West Coast.


I like designing and developing video games that excite people. Seeing people enjoy the games I've worked on is one of the biggest reasons why I like making video games. 
Most games I've made have been in Unity with a small side of Godot. Recently, I was able to work on a couple VR games which have piqued my interest into [XR][xr-def] technologies.


Outside of that, in my free time I like to play video games, go out on hikes, or eat tasty food. Currently, the two games in my rotation are [Slay the Spire 2][sts-2] and [Tekken 8][tk-8].


If you're curious, feel free to explore the rest of my website. I'm not active on social media so please contact me through LinkedIn or my email for any inquiries. Thanks! :smile:
