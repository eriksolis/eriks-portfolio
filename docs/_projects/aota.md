An army of slimes commanded by the evil Gerald has invaded the village. It is up to you, the Apprentice, to save the village from the treacherous horde of slimes.

This game was made for CSC-378, Interactive Entertainment Engineering. It was my final project for the class where I made it with 3 other students. You might be thinking Adventures of the Apprentice 2? Where's Adventures of the Apprentice 1? AOTA 1 was a previous game I made by myself in the same class but in Unity. AOTA 2 was made in Godot as the rest of the group was more familiar with the game engine compared to Unity. The entire project was recreated with new sprites, new abilities, and more enemies. 

My role in the group was to make sure the ideas and story of the game was correctly built off the first one. I did some light programming and level design such as the teleportation system, rooms, and the final boss room.
