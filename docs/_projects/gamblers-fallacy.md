Gino suffers from a crippling gambling addiction. They have fallen so low that they’re in deep debt to the mob. Equipped with their boomeranging gun, they decide to break into the headquarters of the mob and settle their debt. One way or another.

Gambler's Fallacy is a short arcade style shooter game made for my Game Design class. It was a quarter-long project that was made with 4 other students and was the very first game I made in Unity! Through this game and the class I was introduced to the iterative process of making video games such as creating design documents, milestone deadlines, and conducting playtest sessions.  

{% include gallery caption="In-game screenshots." %}

Designing the game was a collaborative endeavor so as for my role in the group I was one of the programmers. I created the core mechanics of the game including the movement and the gun boomerang. I also implemented the slot machine which was the required feedback loop for our game. It was a lot of fun working with other people on a game and also having a dedicated artist helps immensely because that means I could focus on programming :relieved:.
