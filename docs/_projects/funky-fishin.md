**Features:**

* Swing the controllers in given directions to the beat
* Catch a variety of funky fish 
* Listen to some funky music by inserting cassette tapes into a boombox
* DJ Seagull will encourage you throughout your catches!

Funky Fishin' was made for the Meta Quest 3 and built in Unity with Wwise Integration and Meta XR SDK.

Funky Fishin' is a VR Fishing Rhythm game where you must catch funky fish by swinging your controllers to the beat. Funky Fishin' was inspired by VR Rhythm games such as Beat Saber and Samba De Amigo and of course fishing games. 

This was made for my Mixed Reality class and was the first time using a VR headset to create a video game. The university provided each student with a Meta Quest 3 in the class so we used that as the base for our projects throughout the quarter. My role in the two person group was to figure out how to program the core mechanics of the game and level design. The core mechanics being the detection of the controller movements, spawning of the directions the player must swing, and ultimately combining those to be in sync with the music that plays. 

Overall, this game taught me a lot, especially having to optimize so many settings have it run somewhat smoothly on the Meta Quest 3. It was also a lot of fun to learning Wwise and how to integrate that into Unity.
